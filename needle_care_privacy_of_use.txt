Information Collection And Use

We don't collect any type of information from user. 
This skill help to provide the details about the vaccination/immunisation details recommended for the user, add a child and track the vaccination/immunisation recorded history.
User can say Alexa Open Needle care & start asking the details of added child's vaccination/immunisation details. This skill doesn't capture any user data. Also the details provided as part of vaccination/immunisation may not be accurate, it is recommended to check the medicine details with professional physician or Health care provider.
